## How to Customize Copy

### Step 1: Identify Your Audience's Pain
- Are they non-technical founders? (Focus on "no code required")
- Are they developers? (Focus on "clean code, zero dependencies")
- Are they designers? (Focus on "production-ready components")

### Step 2: Pick Your Primary Benefit
- Speed? â†’ "Ship in 30 minutes"
- Quality? â†’ "Look like you raised $5M"
- Ease? â†’ "No design skills required"
- Trust? â†’ "Join 500+ founders"

### Step 3: Match Copy to Benefit
If speed is primary:
- Headline: "Ship Your Landing Page This Weekend"
- Subheadline: "30 minutes from download to deployed"
- CTA: "Start Building Now"

If quality is primary:
- Headline: "Make Your AI Startup Look Funded"
- Subheadline: "Professional sections that build instant trust"
- CTA: "Get Premium Sections"

### Step 4: Test & Iterate
- Launch with one version
- Track conversion rate
- Try new copy after 100 visitors
- Compare results
- Keep what works
